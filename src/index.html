Introduction to UA high
UA High School is a prestigious institution known for cultivating the next generation of exceptional heroes. Our academy boasts a cutting-edge curriculum designed to hone students' quirks and tactical abilities. At UA, we prioritize practical hero training, preparing students for real-world challenges they'll face in their heroic careers. Our distinguished faculty includes experienced Pro Heroes who provide valuable insights and mentorship. The campus facilities are state-of-the-art, offering advanced training grounds and resources for comprehensive education. We seek promising individuals with a strong sense of justice, resilience, and the potential to become outstanding heroes. UA High School is not just a school; it's a launchpad for those aspiring to make a significant impact in the world of heroism. Join us, and together, we'll shape a safer future.
